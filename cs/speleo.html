Free speech of hate

I was selected as part of the Poster Triennial in Lahti, Finland, to take part in a week long poster-a-thon where the theme was 'Freedom of Expression'.

The poster which was selected (Free Speech >< Hate Speech) represents the fine line between freedom of expression and harmful rhetoric. What begins as open discourse gradually distorts into divisive, inflammatory speech, highlighting how unchecked freedom of speech can evolve into hate speech when empathy, accountability, and critical awareness are neglected. Where do we draw the line, and who gets to draw it?
